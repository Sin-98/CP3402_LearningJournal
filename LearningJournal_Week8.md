
## Learning Activities

### Learning Experiment

This week I wanted to find out whether listening to background music helps me concentrate while working on my university assignments.

### Hypothesis

Studying without background music will help me focus better and complete my work more efficiently than studying with background music.

### Experiment

I completed two separate 45-minute study sessions while working on my university assignments.

**Session 1 – With Background Music**

- Listened to instrumental lo-fi music through headphones.
- Worked on my WordPress assignment.
- Recorded how focused I felt, how often I became distracted, and how much work I completed.

**Session 2 – Without Background Music**

- Studied in the same location at a similar time of day.
- Worked on another section of the same assignment.
- Recorded the same observations about focus, distractions, and productivity.

To keep the experiment fair, I used the same study environment, laptop, and study duration for both sessions.

### Results

During the session with background music, I felt more relaxed, but I occasionally paid attention to the music instead of the assignment. I also found myself taking slightly longer to complete some tasks.

During the session without music, I stayed focused for longer periods and completed more work within the same 45 minutes. I needed fewer breaks and found it easier to think through problems.

The results supported my hypothesis that I concentrate better without background music when working on assignments that require problem-solving and careful thinking.

## Learning Insights

This experiment helped me understand that my study environment has a significant impact on my productivity. While background music can make studying feel more enjoyable, it is not always the best choice for assignments that require deep concentration.

In the future, I will study without music when working on coding or WordPress assignments. If I am doing simple or repetitive tasks, I may still use quiet instrumental music because it helps me stay motivated.
