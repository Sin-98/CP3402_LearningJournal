## Content Insights
This week I learned how WordPress differs from Joomla despite both being Content Management Systems. WordPress focuses heavily on themes, plugins, and page-based content management, making it relatively straightforward to create and customise websites without extensive coding knowledge.
One important concept I learned was the distinction between themes and plugins. Themes control the visual appearance of a website, while plugins add additional functionality. Understanding this separation helped me see how WordPress websites can be expanded without modifying the core system. This approach improves maintainability and reduces the risk of breaking the website when updates are applied.
I also learned about the process of installing WordPress on a hosting service. Although the installation itself was simple, understanding databases, file management, and hosting configurations gave me a better understanding of how websites are deployed in a real-world environment. Compared to creating static websites with GitHub Pages, WordPress requires more server-side components but provides significantly more flexibility.

## Career/Employability/Learning Insights
Researching WordPress showed me how widely used the platform remains in industry. Many web development, digital marketing, and content management roles list WordPress experience as a desirable skill. Although custom software development interests me more than website administration, learning WordPress improves my ability to work with clients and businesses that rely on CMS platforms.
From a learning perspective, I noticed that I spent a significant amount of time troubleshooting hosting and installation issues. Initially, I became frustrated because I viewed these problems as obstacles preventing me from completing the task. However, after reading documentation and support resources, I realised that troubleshooting itself is an important learning activity. Understanding how hosting, domains, and CMS platforms interact gave me a much deeper understanding than simply following a tutorial without encountering any problems.
